The Arizer Solo II is the latest vaporizer produced by the well known and respected Canadian manufacturer Arizer. This device is the evolved version of the famous Arizer Solo that we all know and loved. Although there are many similarities to the original Solo, this version has really stepped it up a notch where it counts; such as the LED display, temperature control capabilities, an improved air path system and better battery life.

One of the most significant changes on the new Solo II in the user-friendly interface. The original Solo featured seven temperature indication lights which has been replaced by an LED display on the newer model. Below this new display are the navigation buttons which allows easy control of the customization features and temperature control.

The Solo II allows precise customization through various settings. These settings include volume control, a session timer, temperature measurement options (Celsius or Fahrenheit), LED brightness and a start-up timer. Using these new features is a guaranteed way to improve your vaping experience.

The Solo II provides extreme temperature flexibility which can be set anywhere between 50 and 210 degrees Celsius. The heat up time is also much quicker and more efficient than the previous model. The new Solo II takes less than 30 seconds to reach the maximum temperature, a significant improvement.

Time to brag about vapour quality, a right which the Arizer Solo II has earned. The air path has been improved to make it less restrictive which improves airflow. Also, with the new precision temperature control system, the flavour produced is much more pure and tasteful. Use the lower temperatures to preserve flavour and to create a thinner cloud or increase the temperature for a thicker cloud which reduces some of the flavour but produces no harshness.

The Solo II has twice the battery lifespan of its predecessor and four times more than the original Arizer Air. Depending on your heat setting, the Solo II will provide up to three hours of impressive battery life.

How to Use:

  1. Load Glass Aroma Tube. Do not over pack dish or pack dish too tightly.
  2. Insert the glass aroma tube.
  3. Press and hold the up arrow (^) and the menu (M) button to power the unit on.
  4. Use the arrow buttons to set the desired temperature.
  5. Inhale vapour through the glass aroma tube.

Pro Tip for aroma therapy purposes, use the small dish for dried botanicals (example: lavender flowers) and/or 4-8 drops of essential oils. Set the temperature between 50 C - 100C (122F - 212F) and gently inhale the fragrance.

V-Tower

Hailing from Canada, Arizer set out to make a vaporizer comparable to other well-known units for a fraction of the price. Utilizing true convection, the V-Tower is a convenient and reliable desktop vaporizer. The V-Tower uses glass components perfect for maintaining flavour and keeping clean. The digital readout allows for temperature, volume and C/F control, while the wide base keeps the device sturdy. The exterior of the unit is high grade, dual-wall, stainless steel that stays cool to the touch. However, the glass pieces get hot. Seriously.

Instructions

To use: Press power button to turn ON/OFF. Unit will beep and digital readout will appear. Toggle through precise temperatures by pressing the up and down arrows. Hit the menu button to silence unit to switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit, or set timer. Unit takes about a minute to heat up fully. Remember the element is wrapped in glass, so it will get HOT. It is recommended for first time use to let unit burn off excess materials for roughly 5 minutes before using. Optimal temperature is based on moisture content of your herb, amount being used, whether its fine or coarsely ground, how discreet youre trying to be and of course the type of hit you enjoy. Somewhere between 180-220C is a great place to stay within, remember its always easier to add heat than to remove it. While youre waiting for your unit to heat start packing your bowl, and once heating is completed connect bowl with glass surrounding heating element. After bowl is placed on device ONLY touch the black rim (this black rim should be at the top) the rest of the glass will be TOO HOT. I cannot stress this enough, you WILL burn your fingers. After the bowl is in place add glass whip. Draw slowly to achieve proper hits. Adding heat as your session increases is a great way to utilize all your herb has to offer. When herb is finished it will lose its flavour and will be a dark brown colour, however it will still be the same consistency as when you put it in. Save your vaporized material for making edibles. Vaporizing has decarboxylated the herb for you youre ready to bake!
